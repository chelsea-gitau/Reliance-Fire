SYS·06 · Risk Assessment
FIRE RISK ASSESSMENT

A professional fire risk assessment is a legal requirement for all non-domestic premises in Kenya. Our certified assessors carry out thorough inspections of your property, identify fire hazards, evaluate existing controls and produce a detailed written report with actionable recommendations.

Full Site Survey

Thorough inspection of all areas including roof voids and plant rooms.

Hazard Identification

Systematic identification of ignition sources and fire loads.

Risk Evaluation

Assessment of risk to occupants including vulnerable persons.

Written Report

Detailed report with photographs and prioritised action plan.

Regulatory Compliance

Ensures compliance with Kenya fire safety regulations.

Insurance Support

Reports accepted by all major insurers for premium assessment.
