SYS·02 · Suppression

SPRINKLER SYSTEMS

Sprinkler systems are the most effective automatic fire suppression method available. We design and install wet pipe, dry pipe and pre-action systems tailored to your building type, occupancy and risk level. All installations are fully compliant with BS EN 12845 and NFPA 13 standards.

Wet Pipe Systems

Always charged with water — fastest response for most commercial buildings.

Dry Pipe Systems

For unheated spaces where pipes could freeze.

Pre-Action Systems

Two-step activation — ideal for data centres and archives.

Deluge Systems

Open head systems for high-hazard industrial areas.

Sprinkler Heads

Upright, pendent and concealed heads to suit all aesthetics.

Hydraulic Design

Full hydraulic calculations and documentation provided.
