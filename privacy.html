PRIVACY POLICY

Last Updated: July 2026

Reliance Fire Safety EA Ltd ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what personal data we collect through this website, how we use it, and your rights regarding it, in line with the Kenya Data Protection Act, 2019, and, where applicable, the UK/EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

1. What We Collect

  • Contact form submissions: name, company, email, phone number, and message content.
  • Shop orders: name, contact details, and delivery information needed to fulfil your order.
  • Cart data: stored locally in your browser (localStorage), not transmitted to us until you complete an order.
  • Analytics data (only if you consent via our cookie banner): pages visited, general location, device/browser type.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To respond to enquiries and quotation requests
  • To process and fulfil Shop orders
  • To improve our website and services (analytics, only with consent)
  • To comply with legal obligations

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

3. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your data based on your consent (e.g. submitting a form, accepting analytics cookies), the necessity of processing to fulfil a contract (e.g. an order), or our legitimate business interests (e.g. responding to enquiries).

4. Cookies

We use cookies as described in our Cookie Policy. Non-essential cookies (analytics, marketing) only run after you give consent via our cookie banner.

5. Third-Party Services

We may use trusted third-party services to operate this site (such as form handling or WhatsApp Business messaging). These providers only receive the data necessary to perform their function and are bound by their own privacy terms.

6. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes described above, or as required by Kenyan law (e.g. for tax or contractual records).

7. Your Rights

Depending on applicable law, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict the use of your personal data, and to withdraw consent at any time. To exercise these rights, contact us at info@reliancefireea.com.

8. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Children's Privacy

This site is intended for business and adult use. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last Updated" date above reflects the most recent revision.

11. Contact Us

For any privacy-related questions or requests, email info@reliancefireea.com or visit our Contact page.
