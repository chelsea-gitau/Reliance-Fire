COOKIE POLICY

Last Updated: July 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how Reliance Fire Safety EA Ltd uses cookies and similar technologies on this website, and how you can control them.

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites function, remember preferences, and (with consent) understand how visitors use the site.

2. How We Use Cookies

We group cookies into three categories. Only Necessary cookies run automatically — everything else waits for your explicit consent via the cookie banner or the "Manage Preferences" option, available at any time from the banner.

Necessary Cookies

Required for the site to function correctly — this includes remembering items in your Shop cart and basic security features. These cannot be disabled as the site cannot function properly without them.

Analytics Cookies

Help us understand how visitors use the site (e.g. which pages are viewed most) so we can improve it. These only run if you select "Accept All" or enable Analytics under "Manage Preferences."

Marketing Cookies

Used for advertising and retargeting purposes. Off by default — these only run if you explicitly enable them.

3. Managing Your Preferences

You can change your cookie preferences at any time by clicking "Manage Preferences" on the cookie banner, which appears on your first visit and remains accessible via this policy. You can also clear cookies through your browser settings at any time.

4. Third-Party Cookies

Where we use third-party tools (such as analytics or chat widgets), those providers may set their own cookies once you've given consent for that category. We do not control these providers' cookies directly.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the tools we use or legal requirements.

6. Contact

Questions about our use of cookies can be sent to info@reliancefireea.com.
